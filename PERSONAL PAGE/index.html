FriendGame

A game I made up to play with my friends

This is a game in development. It's a simple card game where you play against your friends. (Well, my friends at least.)
Each card has an HP (health points), an ATK (attack points) and a GV (gold value).
Each player draws 10 cards at the beginning of the game, and may field up to 5 cards at a time. In order to field a card, you must spend its GV cost in gold.

Rules

HOW TO WIN

  • Eliminate all other players by attacking them until their HP drops to zero.

TURNS

  • - Each player turn has THREE stages. -
  • - First stage: Collect all GOLD earned and draw cards. (Replenish hand up to ten cards) -
  • - Second Stage: Deploy cards by paying their GV, or sell deployed cards for half of their GV. -
  • - Third Stage: Choose which cards to activate for your turn. -

ACTIVATING CARDS

  • To activate a card, place it either at the FRONT or BACK of your field.
  • Cards at the FRONT will attack based on their ATK and any modifiers applied.
  • Cards at the BACK use their ability, starting or resetting any cooldown timers required.
  • Any cards that remain in the CENTER of a player's field will be considered inactive for that turn.

ATTACKING

  • When a player attacks with either a HERO or CREATURE cared, they must choose a target.
  • A player can target either an opposing player OR any card on an opposing player's field.
  • Inactive cards are still vulnerable to attacks, even if they are not utilized on a given turn.
  • When a HERO or CREATURE card's HP reaches zero, remove it from the field and place it in the discard pile.
